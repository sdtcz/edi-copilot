Frequently asked questions

How long does implementation take?

Most customers are up and running in under a week. Our white-glove onboarding team handles setup, integration, and training. For complex enterprises, expect 2-4 weeks.

What EDI standards do you support?

We support all major EDI standards including ANSI X12, EDIFACT, XML, and custom formats. Our AI learns your specific requirements and adapts automatically.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes! We integrate with all major ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, etc.), WMS platforms, and custom applications via REST API or direct database connections.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use enterprise-grade encryption. Your data never leaves your designated region, and we offer on-premise deployment for enterprises.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support. Professional plans get priority support with 4-hour response times. Enterprise customers get 24/7 dedicated support with a named account manager.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Our team will help you set up a proof-of-concept with your actual data.
